Rex Dutton
Pulpit Minister

After 44 years in the ministry, six of which were spent in mission work in Africa, I can easily say that this is the finest congregation of which I have ever been a member. This church family is a dedicated group of faithful, friendly, loving, and caring people. Our elders are among the best you will find anywhere in the world. Our church staff is talented, competent and humbly committed to serving the needs of the souls of men.

Every Christian wants to be challenged to grow in their walk with the Lord, and then go to heaven when life is done. My mission is to help as many as possible do just that. Therefore, I am at your service.
